# Market Intelligence: Craft Beer Industry Analysis

## Business Context
Category managers and procurement professionals need market intelligence to make informed sourcing decisions. This project demonstrates **consumer analytics through web scraping and text analysis** of product reviewsâ€”techniques applicable to supplier evaluation, product quality assessment, and market trend identification.

## Project Highlights

### Problem Addressed
Built a **market analysis pipeline** for the craft beer industry to:
- Collect consumer reviews and ratings at scale
- Extract product attributes from unstructured text
- Identify market trends and consumer preferences

### Key Outcomes
- **Consumer Voice Data**: Automated collection of reviews, ratings, and product metadata
- **Attribute Extraction**: NLP-based identification of flavor profiles and quality indicators
- **Trend Analysis**: Data-driven insights into market preferences and emerging categories
- **Scalable Framework**: Reusable pipeline adaptable to other product categories

## Methodology
| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Data Collection** | Web scraping of review sites |
| **Text Processing** | NLP preprocessing, tokenization |
| **Attribute Extraction** | Keyword identification (hoppy, malty, etc.) |
| **Analysis** | Sentiment scoring, trend visualization |
